Burning stubble harms our environment and wastes valuable resources. By connecting farmers with manufacturers, we turn waste into opportunity—reducing pollution, boosting incomes, and building a sustainable future.

Access to sustainable farming shouldn't be a struggle

Yet, thousands of farmers face challenges in managing stubble waste, leading to pollution and lost income. Join us in creating a greener future by transforming stubble into opportunity.

Journey

1️

User Registration

Farmers, Manufacturers, and Admins sign up with secure role-based authentication.

2️

Farmer Stock Listing

Farmers upload detailed stock information, including quantity, location, and pricing.

3️

Manufacturer Browsing

Manufacturers explore available stock based on location and place instant orders.

4️

Order Processing

Farmers recieves orders and prepare for pickup with real-time tracking.

5️

Order Delivered

Manufacturer receives the stubble stock.Order marked as complete, and payment is processed.

6️

Order Completion & Feedback

Farmers & Manufacturers rate each other. Feedback helps improve platform quality.
